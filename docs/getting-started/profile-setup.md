# Profile Setup

Learn how to customize and manage your user profile in the Platform Console.

## Accessing Your Profile

To access your profile settings:

1. Click on your **profile avatar** in the top-right corner of the header
2. Select **"Profile"** from the dropdown menu


## Profile Overview

Your profile page contains several sections for managing your account information:


### Profile Sections

- **Personal Information**: Basic details like name, email, phone
- **Profile Picture**: Your avatar image
- **Security Settings**: Password and authentication options
- **Preferences**: Language, timezone, and notification settings
- **Organization Info**: Current organization details

## Personal Information

### Updating Basic Details


**Editable Fields:**

- **First Name**: Your given name
- **Last Name**: Your family name
- **Email Address**: Primary email (may require verification)
- **Phone Number**: Contact number with country code
- **Job Title**: Your role or position
- **Department**: Your team or department

**To Update Information:**

1. Click the **"Edit"** button next to the information section
2. Modify the fields you want to change
3. Click **"Save Changes"** to confirm
4. You'll see a success notification

### Email Verification

If you change your email address:


1. A verification email will be sent to your new address
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. Your email will be updated once verified
4. You'll remain logged in during this process

## Profile Picture Management

### Uploading a Profile Picture


**Steps:**

1. Click on the current profile picture or placeholder
2. Select **"Upload New Picture"**
3. Choose an image file from your device
4. Crop and position the image as desired
5. Click **"Save"** to apply changes

**Image Requirements:**

- **File formats**: JPG, PNG, GIF
- **Maximum size**: 5MB
- **Recommended resolution**: 256x256 pixels
- **Aspect ratio**: Square (1:1) works best

### Removing Profile Picture

To remove your current profile picture:

1. Click on your profile picture
2. Select **"Remove Picture"**
3. Confirm the action
4. Your profile will show the default avatar

## Security Settings

### Password Management


**Changing Your Password:**

1. Go to the **"Security"** tab in your profile
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password twice
5. Click **"Update Password"**

**Password Requirements:**

- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character
- Cannot be a previously used password

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)


**Enabling 2FA:**

1. In the Security section, find "Two-Factor Authentication"
2. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save the backup recovery codes

**Managing 2FA:**

- **View Backup Codes**: Access your recovery codes
- **Regenerate Codes**: Create new backup codes
- **Disable 2FA**: Turn off two-factor authentication

### Active Sessions


Monitor and manage your login sessions:

- View all devices where you're logged in
- See login times and locations
- Revoke access from specific devices
- End all other sessions for security

## Preferences Settings

### Language and Region


**Available Options:**

- **Language**: Choose your preferred interface language
- **Time Zone**: Set your local timezone for accurate timestamps
- **Date Format**: Select date display format (MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY, etc.)
- **Number Format**: Choose decimal and thousands separators

### Notification Preferences


**Notification Types:**

- **Email Notifications**: Receive alerts via email

  - System updates
  - Device alerts
  - Application notifications
  - Weekly summaries

- **In-App Notifications**: Browser notifications

  - Real-time alerts
  - Message notifications
  - System announcements

- **SMS Notifications**: Text message alerts
  - Critical system alerts
  - Security notifications
  - Emergency contacts

**Frequency Settings:**

- **Immediate**: Get notifications right away
- **Hourly Digest**: Bundled notifications every hour
- **Daily Summary**: Daily digest emails
- **Weekly Report**: Weekly activity summaries

### Dashboard Preferences


Customize your dashboard experience:

- **Default Landing Page**: Choose where to go after login
- **Widget Layout**: Arrange dashboard widgets
- **Data Refresh Rate**: How often to update live data
- **Chart Colors**: Customize chart color schemes

## Organization Information

### Current Organization


View details about your current organization:

- **Organization Name**: Your current organization
- **Your Role**: Your permission level
- **Member Since**: When you joined
- **Department**: Your assigned department
- **Manager**: Your reporting manager (if applicable)

### Switching Organizations

If you belong to multiple organizations:


1. Click the **organization name** in the header
2. Select a different organization from the dropdown
3. Your interface will switch to the selected organization
4. Your permissions may change based on your role in each organization

### Organization Requests


Manage organization invitations:

- **Pending Invitations**: Organizations you've been invited to join
- **Join Requests**: Your requests to join organizations
- **Request Access**: Apply to join a new organization

## Privacy Settings

### Data Privacy


Control your data and privacy:

- **Profile Visibility**: Who can see your profile
- **Activity Tracking**: Allow usage analytics
- **Data Export**: Download your data
- **Account Deletion**: Delete your account

### Communication Preferences


Manage how the platform communicates with you:

- **Marketing Emails**: Product updates and news
- **Survey Invitations**: Feedback requests
- **Event Notifications**: Webinars and events
- **Partner Communications**: Third-party messages

## Account Actions

### Export Account Data


Download your account data:

1. Go to **Privacy Settings**
2. Click **"Export Data"**
3. Select what data to include
4. Choose export format (JSON, CSV, PDF)
5. Request the export
6. Download when ready (you'll receive an email)

### Delete Account


To permanently delete your account:

1. Go to **Privacy Settings**
2. Click **"Delete Account"**
3. Read the warnings carefully
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Type "DELETE" to confirm
6. Your account will be permanently removed

⚠️ **Warning**: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be lost.

## Troubleshooting Profile Issues

### Profile Picture Won't Upload

**Possible Solutions:**

- Check file size (must be under 5MB)
- Try a different image format
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

### Email Changes Not Saving

**Possible Solutions:**

- Check internet connection
- Verify the email format is correct
- Ensure the email isn't already in use
- Contact support if the issue persists

### 2FA Not Working

**Possible Solutions:**

- Check your device's time is correct
- Try generating a new QR code
- Use backup codes if available
- Contact support for assistance

## Best Practices

### Security Best Practices

- **Strong Password**: Use a unique, complex password

### Privacy Best Practices

- **Regular Exports**: Back up important data
- **Monitor Activity**: Review account activity regularly
