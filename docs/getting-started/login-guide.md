# Login Guide

Welcome to the Platform Console! This guide will help you log into the system and get started with your account.

## Accessing the Platform

To access the Platform Console, open your web browser and navigate to the URL provided by your administrator (typically something like `https://platform.yourcompany.com`).

## Login Page Overview

When you first visit the Platform Console, you'll see a beautiful login page with:


### Key Elements

- **Welcome Animation**: An animated welcome section on the left side
- **Login Form**: The main login form on the right side with email and password fields
- **Secure Connection**: Look for the lock icon in your browser's address bar

## How to Login

### Step 1: Enter Your Email

1. Click on the **Email address** field
2. Type your registered email address
3. Make sure there are no typos in your email


### Step 2: Enter Your Password

1. Click on the **Password** field
2. Type your password

### Step 3: Sign In

1. Click the **Sign in** button
2. Wait for the system to verify your credentials
3. You'll see a loading animation while logging in


## After Successful Login

Once you successfully log in, you'll be redirected to the **Dashboard** where you can:

- View your applications and devices
- Access your profile settings
- Manage your organization
- Create new applications and devices


## Login Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Invalid email or password" Error

- **Double-check your email**: Make sure you're using the correct email address
- **Verify your password**: Passwords are case-sensitive
- **Check for typos**: Ensure there are no extra spaces or characters


#### "Account not found" Message

- Contact your system administrator to verify your account is created
- Check if you're using the correct email address
- Ensure your account hasn't been deactivated

#### Browser Issues

- **Clear your browser cache**: Go to Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data
- **Try a different browser**: Test with Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- **Disable browser extensions**: Some ad blockers might interfere with login

#### Network Issues

- Check your internet connection
- If you're behind a corporate firewall, contact your IT department
- Try accessing from a different network

## First Time Login

If this is your first time logging in:

1. You may be prompted to change your temporary password
2. Complete your profile information
3. Set up two-factor authentication (if required)
4. Review and accept terms of service

## Security Features

### Password Requirements

Your password must contain:

- At least 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Account Security

- The system automatically logs you out after 24 hours of inactivity
- Failed login attempts will temporarily lock your account
- All login activities are logged for security purposes

## Need Help?

2. **Check Documentation**: Review the [Common Issues](../help/common-issues) section
3. **Password Reset**: Use the "Forgot Password" feature if available
