# Inventory Management Overview

Comprehensive inventory management system for tracking assets, equipment, and resources across your organization. Manage physical and digital assets with real-time visibility and automated workflows.

## What is Inventory Management?

Inventory Management provides centralized tracking and control of all organizational assets including:

- **Physical Assets**: Hardware, equipment, and devices
- **Digital Assets**: Software licenses, certificates, and digital resources
- **Consumables**: Supplies, parts, and materials
- **Infrastructure**: Servers, network equipment, and facilities
- **Mobile Assets**: Vehicles, portable equipment, and tools

## Core Features

### Asset Tracking

- **Real-time Visibility**: Live status and location tracking
- **Barcode/QR Scanning**: Quick asset identification and updates
- **RFID Integration**: Automated tracking with RFID tags
- **GPS Tracking**: Location tracking for mobile assets
- **Serial Number Management**: Unique identifier tracking

### Inventory Control

- **Stock Levels**: Current quantities and availability
- **Reorder Points**: Automated reorder notifications
- **Procurement**: Purchase order creation and tracking
- **Receiving**: Incoming inventory management
- **Distribution**: Asset allocation and deployment

### Asset Lifecycle

- **Acquisition**: Asset procurement and onboarding
- **Deployment**: Assignment and configuration
- **Maintenance**: Scheduled and reactive maintenance
- **Monitoring**: Performance and utilization tracking
- **Disposal**: End-of-life asset management
